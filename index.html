Perfectly Positioned for Your Mount Kenya Adventure

Our Location

Situated in the tranquil town of Naro Moru, our hotel provides easy access to Mount Kenya National Park through the Naro Moru Gate. We are approximately 3 hours from Nairobi by car, with scenic views throughout the journey.

Directions

From Nairobi: Take the Nairobi-Nanyuki road and turn left at Naro Moru town center. Follow signs to Mount Kenya National Park, Naro Moru Gate.

Accessibility

The hotel is accessible by both private and public transport. We offer airport transfer services from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO) upon request.
